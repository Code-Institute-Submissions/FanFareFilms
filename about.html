About Us.

Who we are, what we do and where you can find us.

Who We Are

We're a few film fanatics who got together three years ago to change things up.

Seeing new films is great but we so rarely see our favourites in the way that they were meant to be seen. Big, Loud, Comfortable.

So we bought an old cinema, styled it to our taste and regularly bring our chosen classics to our like-minded customers.

Hidden gems are everywhere though, which is why we set up a system so you can tell us what our community should be experiencing.

Let's experience the greats together.

What We Do

Step 1: Book in to see a classic

We have four screens showcasing four legendary titles all week.

Everything snacks wise you'd expect - popcorn, chocolate and big drinks. All reasonably priced like our films.

Comfy seats, space to stretch out and favourite films as they were meant to be seen.

Step 2: Have your say

Choose your next adventure. On the vote page we have suggestions that we and other movie fanatics have made.

Cast your vote for what you want to see upcoming.

We tally the votes and get them into our next line up. Updated over the weekend for your next weeks viewing pleasure.

Should none of those films take your fancy, make a suggestion and let us know what you think.

Step 3: Rinse and Repeat

Book, View, Enjoy, Vote. That's our system.

What we're really trying to do is build a community of people who enjoy our favourite hobby.

So don't be shy - speak up, join us, view the legendary performances how the directors of old envisoned it, alongside friends.
